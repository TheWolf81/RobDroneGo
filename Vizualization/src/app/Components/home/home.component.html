Welcome to RobDroneGo

What is RobDroneGo?

RobDroneGo is a web solution, developed by 3rd year Informatics Engeneering students in ISEP, based in NodeJS, Angular and other technologies.

It came to life with the purpose of aiding the newly acquired fleet of Robots and Drones


What can RobDroneGo do?

RobDroneGo can help you manage your fleet of Robots and Drones, by providing a web interface where you can manage ISEP's Campus data, Robot data, and request tasks for the robots to perform.

It also provides a way to visualize, in 3D, some of the Campus' components, such as building floors and even control a robot within the premises.

The features available to you depend on your role, whether you are a student, a campus manager, a fleet manager, an administrator or a task manager
